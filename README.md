# 🪢 Tug of Mathematics

**Tug of Mathematics** is a browser-based educational game for children. Players solve math problems to "pull the rope" in a tug-of-war against an AI opponent. Correct answers move the rope toward the player, and faster answers increase the pull strength. The game tracks combos, rounds and series scores, making it engaging and competitive.

## 🎮 Features

- **Player vs AI**: Turn-based gameplay with AI opponent.  
- **Best-of-3 Rounds**: Each series consists of up to 3 rounds.  
- **Visual Feedback**:  
  - Correct/Wrong answers visible for player and AI  
  - “Almost there!” warning when the rope is near the target  
  - Combo tracker to reward consecutive correct answers  
- **Win Animations**: Rope flashes on round wins.  
- **Child-Friendly UI**: Simple, soft colors, horizontally and vertically centered layout.  
- **Responsive Design**: Works on both desktop and mobile browsers.  
- **Audio Feedback**: Sounds play on correct and wrong answers.

## 🕹 How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in a web browser.  
2. Solve the math problem displayed on the screen and submit your answer.  
3. Correct answers pull the rope toward you; faster answers pull harder.  
4. Wrong answers **do not affect the opponent** but reset your combo.  
5. Watch the "Almost there!" warning when the rope is close to the winning side.  
6. The first player to win 2 out of 3 rounds wins the series.  
7. Leaderboard displays the score after each round.
